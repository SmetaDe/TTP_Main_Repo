Welcome to the online bakery page!

In 2003, a pair of friends from business school who shared a passion for sweets and a common dream of baking embarked on a remarkable journey. Over the course of 15 years, they dedicated themselves to perfecting their craft and established a bakery that would become a beloved destination for dessert enthusiasts. Today, every delectable treat is meticulously handcrafted on-site, filling their charming 1940s-style stores with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked cakes and pies. Stepping into these delightful establishments evokes nostalgic memories of Grandma's kitchen, evoking a warm and comforting feeling that captures the essence of their bakery's ethos.
